# Marketplace Intelligence Engine v1

## Purpose

The Marketplace Intelligence Engine observes marketplace activity and generates insights for admins, creators, and future recommendation systems.

It does not publish products, change prices, or modify creator data by itself. It turns marketplace signals into useful guidance.

## 1. Market Trends

The engine should observe broad marketplace movement.

Signals:

- Popular products
- Rising demand
- Falling demand
- Seasonal products

These insights help the platform understand what customers are currently interested in and where creators may have new opportunities.

## 2. Search Intelligence

The engine should analyze search behavior.

Signals:

- Most searched keywords
- Missing products
- Unsuccessful searches

Unsuccessful searches are especially important because they reveal demand that the marketplace cannot yet satisfy.

## 3. Creator Insights

The engine should identify creator-side patterns.

Signals:

- Best performing categories
- Average completion score
- Common recipe problems

These insights help admins and creators improve product quality, recipe readiness, and publishing success.

## 4. Customer Insights

The engine should identify customer preference patterns.

Signals:

- Favorite materials
- Preferred price ranges
- Packaging preferences
- Personalization demand

Customer insights help creators build better offers and help the platform guide product development.

## 5. Recommendation Engine

Marketplace intelligence can feed recommendations.

Examples:

- Recommend better pricing.
- Recommend additional photos.
- Recommend engraving.
- Recommend gift packaging.
- Recommend bundle products.

Recommendations should be explainable and based on observed marketplace signals.

## 6. Marketplace Health

The engine should summarize overall marketplace health.

Signals:

- Overall activity
- Growth
- New creators
- Returning customers

Marketplace Health helps admins understand whether the ecosystem is growing, active, and balanced.

## Boundaries

The Marketplace Intelligence Engine provides insights only.

It must not:

- Change product data automatically.
- Modify pricing directly.
- Publish or unpublish products.
- Replace creator or admin decisions.
- Store implementation-specific database rules in the specification.
