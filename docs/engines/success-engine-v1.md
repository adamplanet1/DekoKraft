# Success Engine v1

## Purpose

The Success Engine measures creator success and recommends the next actions that help a creator grow.

It observes creator progress, product performance, customer satisfaction, delivery reliability, and marketplace activity. It should provide clear guidance without changing products or business data automatically.

## 1. Creator Score

Creator Score is an overall measure of creator health and growth readiness.

It can be based on:

- Recipe quality
- Product quality
- Customer satisfaction
- Delivery reliability
- Marketplace activity

The score should help creators understand where they are strong and where they can improve next.

## 2. Product Success

Product Success measures how well individual products perform in the marketplace.

Measures:

- Views
- Favorites
- Conversion
- Repeat purchases

These signals help identify strong products, weak listings, and opportunities for better presentation or offers.

## 3. Growth Recommendations

The engine should recommend practical next actions.

Examples:

- Improve photos.
- Offer engraving.
- Create bundles.
- Reduce delivery time.
- Improve packaging.

Recommendations should be specific, understandable, and connected to measurable success signals.

## 4. Milestones

Milestones help creators see progress and stay motivated.

Examples:

- First Product
- First Sale
- 10 Sales
- 100 Sales
- Trusted Creator
- Master Creator

Milestones can unlock future support, visibility, learning paths, or partner opportunities.

## 5. Success Dashboard

The Success Dashboard should display:

- Overall Score
- Current Level
- Achievements
- Recommended Next Step

The dashboard should focus on encouragement and clarity. It should help creators know what to do next without overwhelming them.

## Boundaries

The Success Engine provides measurement and guidance only.

It must not:

- Modify products automatically.
- Publish or unpublish products.
- Change pricing directly.
- Replace admin quality review.
- Store implementation-specific backend or database rules in this specification.
