# Partner / Creator Ecosystem Blueprint v1

## 1. Purpose

DekoKraft can support Customers, Creator Partners, and Makers in one connected ecosystem.

Customers discover and order products. Creator Partners bring ideas, designs, and products. Makers help produce, customize, or fulfill orders. Admins keep the platform safe, organized, and high quality.

The goal is to help creative people turn products and skills into real business opportunities.

## 2. Roles

### Customer

A person who browses, customizes, orders, or requests products and services through DekoKraft.

### Creator Partner

A partner who owns a product, design, idea, or creative offering and wants support with visibility, product pages, marketing, orders, and growth.

### Maker

A person or workshop that can manufacture, customize, repair, package, or fulfill products.

### Admin

The platform operator responsible for review, quality control, publishing, partner support, order oversight, and ecosystem health.

## 3. Core Principle

The platform does not compete with partners; it helps them succeed.

DekoKraft should provide tools, structure, visibility, guidance, and operational support so partners can grow without losing ownership of their products or creative identity.

## 4. Partner Journey

### Register

The partner joins the platform and provides basic identity, contact, and business information.

### Submit Product

The partner submits a product, design, service, or idea for review.

### Improve Product Page

The platform helps improve product title, description, images, pricing, categories, and readiness for publishing.

### Get Marketing Suggestions

The partner receives practical guidance for channels, positioning, audience, pricing, and presentation.

### Receive Orders

The partner receives customer orders through the platform with clear order details, support, and fulfillment expectations.

### Grow Into a Small Business

Over time, the partner can improve quality, expand offerings, build repeat customers, and grow into a stronger small business.

## 5. Future Engines

- Partner Success Engine
- Customer Success Engine
- Growth Engine
- Opportunity Engine
- Community Engine

## 6. Rules

- Partners remain owners of their products.
- Platform provides tools, visibility, guidance, and order support.
- Commission / fees must be clear and transparent.
- Quality review is required before publishing partner products.
