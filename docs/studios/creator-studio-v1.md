# Creator Studio Blueprint v1

## Purpose

Creator Studio is the complete workspace where creators build, improve, validate, and publish products.

It gives creators one place to manage product readiness, recipes, content, pricing, packaging, and publishing status.

## 1. Dashboard

The Dashboard shows the creator's overall workspace health.

### Progress

Shows how far the creator has moved through onboarding, product setup, recipe completion, and publishing preparation.

### Completion

Shows how complete the creator's products and recipes are.

### Readiness

Shows whether products are ready for review, marketplace publishing, or further improvement.

### Active Recipes

Lists recipes currently being created, edited, reviewed, or prepared for publishing.

## 2. Product Builder

Product Builder is where creators prepare product information and recipe inputs.

Sections:

### Identity

Product name, category, studio, product code, short description, and creator brand.

### Photos

Product images, lifestyle images, detail images, packaging images, and quality checks.

### Materials

Supported materials, material limits, color / finish options, and material notes.

### Dimensions

Available sizes, custom size rules, measurements, units, and production constraints.

### Configuration

Customer-editable options such as size, material, decoration, text, image upload, quantity, and special requests.

### Pricing

Pricing variables, visible price, internal minimums, packaging cost, margin, and future negotiation inputs.

### Packaging

Packaging type, protection requirements, gift options, branding, and shipping preparation.

### Shipping

Shipping readiness, package dimensions, delivery notes, handling rules, and possible restrictions.

## 3. AI Assistant

The AI Assistant helps creators improve product quality and readiness.

It can help creators:

- Detect missing information
- Suggest better descriptions
- Improve titles
- Estimate pricing
- Detect weak photos
- Recommend packaging

The assistant provides guidance. It should not publish products, override validation, or change business rules without review.

## 4. Validation Panel

The Validation Panel shows whether a product and recipe are ready.

### Recipe Score

Shows how complete the recipe is based on required recipe sections.

### Marketplace Ready

Shows whether the product can move toward publishing.

### Guardian Warnings

Shows safety, quality, missing-data, pricing, or publishing warnings that need attention before the product goes live.

## 5. Publishing

Publishing moves a product through clear lifecycle states.

### Draft

The product is being created or improved.

### Ready

The product has enough information and has passed validation checks for review or publishing.

### Published

The product is visible and sellable in the marketplace.

### Archived

The product is no longer active but remains stored for history, future updates, or reference.

The publishing flow should protect product quality, creator ownership, and customer trust.
